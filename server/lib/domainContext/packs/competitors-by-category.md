This is a per-category map of the players Marico competes with. Use it to interpret share / mix questions, to recognise competitor brand names if they appear in the dataset, and to understand which competitive set matters for which Marico pillar.

## Coconut oil and value-added hair oils

- **Parachute** competes with regional unbranded coconut oil (loose oil), and with branded competitors such as **Shalimar Coconut Oil**, **Klf Nirmal**, **VVD**, **Oachira**, and the smaller **Dabur Anmol**.
- **Value-added hair oils** (Parachute Advansed, Nihar Naturals, Hair & Care) compete with **Bajaj Almond Drops**, **Dabur Vatika** and **Dabur Amla**, **Emami Kesh King** and **Navratna**, **Patanjali Kesh Kanti**, **Himalaya** hair oils and a long tail of regional Ayurvedic brands.
- **Livon** sits in leave-in serum / conditioner against **Streax**, **Kérastase**, **L'Oréal Elvive** serums, and the premium import set in beauty retail.

## Edible oils (Saffola territory)

- **Adani Wilmar (Fortune)**, **Ruchi Soya / Patanjali (Mahakosh, Ruchi Gold, Nutrela)**, **Cargill (Sundrop, Nature Fresh)**, **Emami Agrotech (Healthy & Tasty)**, **Mother Dairy (Dhara)**, **ITC (Aashirvaad svasti / Aashirvaad Atta — adjacent)**.
- Refined oils are commodity-exposed; share moves are tightly linked to retail price, festival promotions and trade margins.

## Foods (Saffola Foods territory)

- **Oats** — **Quaker (PepsiCo)**, **Bagrry's**, **Kellogg's**, **MTR**, store brands.
- **Honey** — **Dabur Honey**, **Patanjali**, **Apis Himalaya**, **Hitkari**, **Beez** and others.
- **Peanut butter / spreads** — **Pintola**, **Sundrop Peanut Butter**, **MyFitness**, **Alpino**.
- **Better-for-you snacks / muesli / breakfast** — **Yogabar**, **RiteBite**, **Kellogg's**, **Nestlé**, **Bagrry's**.
- **Premium nutrition (FITTIFY-adjacent)** — **MuscleBlaze**, **Optimum Nutrition**, **GNC**, **Oziva**, **Wellbeing Nutrition**.

## Male grooming (Set Wet, Beardo)

- **Deodorants** — **Wild Stone (McNROE)**, **Park Avenue (Raymond)**, **Fogg**, **Axe (HUL)**, **Old Spice (P&G)**, **Engage (ITC)**, **Spinz**.
- **Beard care / male skincare** — **The Man Company**, **Bombay Shaving Company**, **Ustraa**, **Beardhood**, **MensXP Mud**.
- **Hair gels / styling** — **Brylcreem (Godrej)**, **Old Spice**, store brands.

## Premium personal care (Just Herbs)

- **Mama Earth (Honasa)**, **Plum**, **The Body Shop**, **Forest Essentials**, **Kama Ayurveda**, **Biotique**, **Khadi**, **WOW Skin Science**.

## Wellness / D2C nutrition (Plix, True Elements)

- **Yogabar**, **Open Secret**, **Saffron Soul**, **Whole Truth**, **Soulful (Tata Soulfull)**, **Nourish You**.

## Patterns to keep in mind

- Competitive context shifts sharply by category — a weakness in haircare doesn't translate to weakness in foods, and competitors don't overlap.
- For coconut oil, **loose / unbranded oil** is the largest "competitor" by volume in many states, especially during commodity-price spikes.
- For male grooming, **regional and acquisition-driven entrants** appear constantly; treat the competitive set as fluid.
- When a question names a competitor brand, look up which category it lives in before drawing conclusions.
